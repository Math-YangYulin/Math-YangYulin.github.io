若 ∀α∈Fn,α′Aα≥0\forall \boldsymbol{\alpha} \in F^{n}, \boldsymbol{\alpha}'\boldsymbol{A}\boldsymbol{\alpha} \geq 0∀α∈Fn,α′Aα≥0.
